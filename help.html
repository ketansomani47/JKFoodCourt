Welcome to JK Food Court

Frequently Asked Qustions


Q. What is JK Food Court?
A. JK Food Court is an online food ordering website. It lets customers consequently place an order for delivery of food.
Q. Do i get charged for this service?
A. No, JK Food Court is a free service.
Q. How is ordering online better than ordering on phone?
A. Ordering online is better than ordering on phone in many ways. Firstly, you get to browse a variety of menus and don't have to collect menu of restaurant. Secondly, we help you make the right choice by providing customer reviews and ratings. Thirdly, you don't have to wait in queue when restaurant phones are engaged and also, there is no communication problem. Finally, you can place advance orders and forget about them and they'll be delivered at your doorstep.
Q. Whom do I contact in case of delay or any other problem with the food?
A. In case of a delay you should immediately call JK Food Court (9571672807)and it will take the necessary steps.
Q. How do I cancel the order?
A. The order can only be cancelled before the restaurant has started preparing it. In case of a cancellation, you need to contact the Jk Food Court.
Q. How can I pay for my order?
A. We accept cash on delivery. In addition, there are various online payment methods available.

Cash on Delivery

Select ‘Cash on Delivery’ on the checkout page and pay the driver at your doorstep when you receive your order.

Credit Card

Select ‘Credit Card’ on the checkout page. After placing your order you will be redirected to the secure payment page of our payment partner, where you can follow the instructions. Once the payment is confirmed, the order will be transmitted to the restaurant.
Q. Are the prices mentioned online different from the ones mentioned in the actual restaurant menu?
A. No, all the prices are exactly the same
Q. I have a voucher card.How can i use it?
A. If you have a voucher code, you can redeem it after selecting a restaurant and adding items to your basket. You will see a field to enter your voucher code on the order overview page. If the voucher is valid, the discount on your order will be calculated immediately. Only one voucher can be used per order.

If your voucher code does not work, feel free to contact us and we’ll take a look.
Q. Where is my order?
A. Our restaurants do everything they can to get your food delivered as quickly as possible. However, sometimes restaurants receive very large amount of orders, or drivers get stuck in heavy traffic - this unfortunately might cause delays.

If the amount of time you’ve waited has exceeded the estimated delivery time, you can contact us and we’ll look into what’s going on.
Q. How to order online at JK Food Court?
A. It is really easy, as easy as 1, 2, 3:
1. Entering Process:If you are regular customer then login otherwise first create an account then login .
2. Choose what you would like: select items you’d like to order. You can search by cuisine type, dish name or by keyword.
3. Checkout: Enter your exact delivery address, payment method and your phone number. Always make sure that you enter the correct phone number to help us contact you regarding your order, if needed.

Now sit back, relax, and we’ll get your food delivered to your doorstep.
Q. I need to change or cancle my order!How can i do this?
A. Please give us a call as soon as possible, before we start preparing your order.

With regards to any refund of a payment you have made online, please contact JK Food Court.
Q. What are your opening hours?
A. JK Food Court is open from 11 am to 11 pm from Monday to Sunday.

During closing hours you can place an order for later.
Q. I don't remember my password?
A. You have already created an account but you can't remember your password? Click on 'Login' at the top of the page. Then click on 'Forgot Password?'. Fill out your email address and a password recovery link will be sent to you by email.
